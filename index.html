Morris Dance Music Quiz

Listen to the following music clips and work out the name and tradition of the Morris dance. The music clips are all taken from the dances in the repertoire for this season. They can start anywhere in the dance and only include a few bars or notes of music. Hopefully, some are very easy. Others are more of a challenge. Good luck and have fun.

Collaboration is encouraged via phone, email, WhatsApp, Messenger etc. Clues can be provided if you get stuck for a suitable bribe.

The results will be published next Sunday 19th April 2020.

The prize is a virtual Easter Egg.
